How Our Contamination Detection Works

A seamless integration with your existing recycling workflow

1

Material Scanning

High-resolution cameras and sensors scan materials as they move along the conveyor belt, capturing detailed images from multiple angles.

2

AI Classification

Our deep learning algorithms analyze the scanned images in real-time, identifying and classifying materials with 95%+ accuracy.

3

Contaminant Removal

Automated sorting arms or targeted air jets remove identified contaminants from the recycling stream within milliseconds.

4

Data Logging

All detection events are logged with timestamps and images, creating a comprehensive database for quality control and reporting.

5

Operator Feedback

Facility staff receive real-time alerts and detailed reports to monitor system performance and contamination trends.
